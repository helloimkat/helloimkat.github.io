CramCase

Description

Cramcase is a project for modernizing and bridging the inefficiencies between students asking questions and professor answering them.

Role

UI / UX Design | 2018 | Freelance


This project is still in works. The case study is gonna be updated as the product progresses.

The Challenge

My part was a reimagination of what a workflow between students and professors might look like. Instead of long, slow and inefficient emails, the platform would allow students to ask questions and professors to answer them either through a message or a short video response, which would be published to a learning management system and available to all students enrolled into the course.

The Solution

The platform is split into two parts - the student dashboard and professor dashboard.

Student Dashboard

The core functionality for the student dashboard is for students of a specific class to be able to interact and view questions that have been asked by their peers and answered by the unit professor, TA or any other authorized official.

The navigation bar has an option for sorting all the questions by either time (most recent, oldest) or topic, and performing a queried lettered search to find any other relevant answers (if no relevant answers are found the system prompts the user to submit their new question).

Question tab

Each unit (class) also comes with the basic information and a schedule which displays lecture times, computer labs, and future exams, assignments, as well as office hours in which students will be the most guaranteed that their questions will be answered.

Information tab - course info, schedule

Students are allowed to upload their personal notes with an option of keeping them private or sharing it with others. If the unit allows, online upload of assignments with a timed and updated tracker (completed, in progress, past due) is available.

Files - file and assignment upload and preview

Professor Dashboard

The default dashboard view for professors parallels the student one. It displays the feed of questions and filters them by first unanswered and then most recent. Each question comes with an option of a text reply or a short (1 - 3min) video answer giving students more personal feel and connection.

Professor dasboard - quesions

Answer options - video recording, text

The same as with the student dashboard, the navigatio bar comes with the sorting option "by time" as well as for filtering between answered and unanswered questions.
